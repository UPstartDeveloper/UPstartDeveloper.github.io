About Me

Hello again! My name's Zain Raza, I am an computer science student at Dominican University of California.

My work focuses on building technology that adapts to humans, and eliminating the need for the latter to adapt to the former. During my senior year at Dominican I built the backend and machine learning features while interning with the team at Carbon0 Games, which introduced me to how AI can simplify the challenges in addressing issues such as climate change. I revel in complexity across the stack - most recently I familiarized myself with Tensorflow.js while integrating computer vision into an empathetic robot; and with the Angular web framework as a summer 2021 intern at PTC, helping to modernize the codebase for Windchill.

I am most passionate about seeing how deep learning can enhance our relationship with each other, and ultimately be put to impactful projects in areas such as climate change.

Oh, and for hobbies I enjoy reading, playing basketball, and producing a podcast about technology, In the Making.
