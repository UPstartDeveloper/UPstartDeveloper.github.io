About Me

Hello again! My name's Zain Raza, I am an incoming Software Development Intern at PTC.

Before PTC I joined Make School, an accelerated two-year program where I am pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Applied Computer Science. In school I built a portfolio of projects around impact issues using my web development skills. During my senior year I built the backend and machine learning features while working with the team at Carbon0 Games, which introduced me to how data-driven decisions can help tackle climate change.

This summer I will be using my web development skills to modernize Windchill, a web-based SaaS product by PTC which helps global industrial manufacturers better trailblaze a way for us out of extinction. I hope to contribute to this industry by making it more sustainable as well as - well, industrious - through machine learning, so that our species can have products to both live better and better tackle climate change.

Oh, and for hobbies I enjoy reading, playing basketball, and producing a podcast about technology, In the Making.
