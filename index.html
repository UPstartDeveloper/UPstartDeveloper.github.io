About Me

Hello again! My name's Zain Raza, and I am an receent CS grad and software developer.

My work focuses on building technology that closes the gap between minds and machines, with the goal of making it easier for the two to collaborate to solve hard problems. During my senior year in college, I built the backend and machine learning features while interning with the team at Carbon0 Games, which introduced me to how AI can address challenges such as climate change. Today, I revel in complex technical problems across the stack, especially related to computer vision - from building an empathetic robot with Tensorflow.js, to deploying a REST API to detect forest fires.

In the future, I look forward to a world where we feel more informed, empowered, and connected with each other thanks to our devices (as opposed to less).

Oh, and for hobbies I enjoy reading, playing basketball, and producing a podcast about technology, In the Making.
